# Jandy VS FloPro Variable-Speed Pump — Owner's Manual

> **Representative excerpt for development.** Structure and fault codes mirror the
> Jandy VS FloPro family for building/testing the assistant. Verify against the

Brand: Jandy · Model: VS FloPro · Document: H0574100

## Overview

The VS FloPro is a variable-speed pool pump. The control interface sets speeds
and schedules and shows any active fault code.

## Operation

Program up to eight speeds and a schedule. Run a lower speed for everyday
filtration and a higher speed for cleaning or features.

## Fault Codes

Fault codes indicate conditions that stopped the drive. Resolve the condition;
the code clears when normal operation resumes.

### Fault Code 410 — Drive Overheat

The drive temperature is too high. Confirm the pump is not in direct sun without
ventilation, clear any obstruction around the motor fan, and let it cool.

### Fault Code 420 — Over Current / Locked Rotor

The motor is drawing too much current, often from a blocked impeller. Power off
at the breaker, clear the pump basket and impeller of debris, then restore power.

## Priming

Ensure the pump basket is full of water and the lid is sealed before starting.
A pump that will not prime usually has an air leak on the suction side.

## Safety

Disconnect power at the breaker before servicing the pump. Never open the pump
under pressure — stop the pump and release pressure at the filter first.

## Specifications

Total horsepower: 2.7 THP. Voltage: 230 VAC. Eight programmable speeds.
