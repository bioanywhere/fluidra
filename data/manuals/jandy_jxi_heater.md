# Jandy JXi Pool & Spa Heater — Owner's Manual

Brand: Jandy · Model: JXi · Document: H0608600

## Overview

The JXi is a gas-fired pool and spa heater. The control board shows the water
temperature, setpoint, and any active fault code on the display.

## Operation

Set the desired temperature with the Up/Down buttons and select Pool or Spa
mode. The heater fires only when adequate water flow and a call for heat are
present.

## Fault Codes

Fault codes indicate conditions that stopped or prevented heating. Resolve the
condition; most codes clear automatically once normal operation resumes.

### Fault Code 301 — Ignition Lockout

The heater tried to light but did not detect a flame. Confirm the gas supply is
on and other gas appliances work. Ignition components must be inspected by a
qualified technician — do not attempt gas repairs yourself.

### Fault Code 305 — Insufficient Water Flow

The heater does not detect enough water flow. Confirm the pump is running, clean
the pump and filter, and ensure valves are open.

### Fault Code 310 — High Limit Temperature

The water temperature exceeded the safe high limit. Heating is disabled until
the water cools. Check for low flow or a stuck valve.

## Maintenance

Keep the area around the heater clear of debris and combustible materials.
Inspect the venting annually.

## Safety

This is a gas appliance. If you smell gas, do not operate any switches — leave
the area immediately and call your gas provider or emergency services. Never
attempt to repair gas lines, valves, or the burner yourself; contact a qualified
technician.

## Specifications

Models: 200k / 260k / 400k BTU. Fuel: natural gas or propane. Minimum water
flow: per model rating.
