# Polaris Alpha iQ+ Robotic Pool Cleaner — Owner's Manual

> **Representative excerpt for development.** Structure and error codes mirror the

Brand: Polaris · Model: Alpha iQ+ · Document: POL-ALPHAIQ

## Overview

The Alpha iQ+ is a robotic pool cleaner controlled through the iAquaLink app.
It runs cleaning cycles, climbs walls, and returns to the waterline. Status and
error codes appear in the app and on the power supply LED.

## Operation

Place the cleaner in the pool, ensure the cable is free of tangles, and start a
cycle from the app. The unit detects water and will not run dry.

## Error Codes

Error codes indicate conditions the cleaner detected. Resolve the condition and
restart the cycle; the code clears on the next successful run.

### Error Code 210 — Out of Water

The cleaner does not detect enough water around the sensors. Confirm the unit is
fully submerged and the sensors are clean, then restart the cycle.

### Error Code 215 — Drive Motor Obstruction

The drive track or impeller is obstructed. Power off the power supply, remove
debris from the tracks and impeller, clear the filter canister, and restart.

### Error Code 220 — Communication Error

The cleaner cannot reach the power supply or the app. Check the cable connection
at the power supply, move the router closer if using Wi-Fi, and power-cycle the
unit.

## Filter Maintenance

Remove and rinse the filter canister after each cycle. Replace the filter media
when cleaning no longer restores flow.

## Safety

Always unplug the power supply before handling the cleaner. Keep the power supply
out of standing water and under a covered, ventilated area.

## Specifications

Cable length: 70 ft. Power supply: 100–240 VAC input, low-voltage output to the
cleaner. Cycle times: 1.5h / 2.5h quick and deep modes.
